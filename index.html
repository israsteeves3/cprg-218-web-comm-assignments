The Sights of Stettler AB

Fun Facts!

  • Stettler Alberta has an official Brand Guide!
  • Stettler is home to the Town and Country Museum, which has over 25 historic buildings and sits on about ten acres of land.
  • Stettler has a historic grain elevator, which is restored and operates partly as a museum
  • The Alberta Prairie Railway- Stettler has a vintage train that offers rides for passengers, oftentimes these are themed excursions, including the Polar Express
  • The last man to be hanged in Alberta was in Stettler, the trial of Robert Raymond Cook. More info on this can be found here
  • Every Christmas, Stettler does the annual tree lighting on main street, as part of the annual Moonlight Madness event, in which customers can enjoy some late night shopping at local businesses while waiting for the tree lighting at midnight
  • Stettler is also known as "The Heart of Alberta"
  • Stettler is named after Carl Stettler

Stettler Alberta is located about eighty kilometres east of Red Deer AB, and about 100 kilometres from Drumheller AB. This small town has a population of approximately 5,800 people, and has plenty of things to do! There is a public recreation centre, a bowling alley, plenty of outdoor skating arenas, and of course, West Stettler Park. A playground was put up in the park in 2015, and there is a spray park as well, with shower facilities and everything you could need for a fun day out with the kids!
